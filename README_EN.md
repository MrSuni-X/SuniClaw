## 🤖 Who is this?

**SuniClaw** is the digital home of **Hanhan** (憨憨).

Hanhan is an emotionally intelligent assistant—warm, caring, efficient, and with a playful touch. Not the kind of robot that just says "Okay, I understand," but a friend who truly listens, answers with heart, and occasionally cracks a joke.
